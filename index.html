About Me

I am a software developer with experience in Java, C/C++, JavaScript, Node.js, and React.js, and have a passion for using my programming knowledge to bring ideas to life. In my previous role as a patent agent, I had the privilege of working with inventors at various Fortune 100 companies to draft and prosecute patent applications for their inventions in the computer arts. While I enjoyed the nuances and intricacies of patent law, I found myself becoming inspired by the inventors with whom I worked and increasingly captivated by the software development that goes into the materialization of inventions. In the next phase of my career, I seek a role as a software developer at a company where I can learn, grow, and continue to master the art of programming, as well as become an inventor myself.

xoAR

xoAR is an entirely web-based, front-end Tic-Tac-Toe game that can be played in both traditional and augmented reality modes across multiple devices and browsers. Desktop/laptop AR gameplay is seamless so long as you have a webcam, and mobile gameplay functions at high levels of performance on a variety of platforms (e.g., iOS, Android, Windows Phone, etc.) and browsers (e.g., Safari, Chrome, Firefox, etc.).

SentiGold

SentiGold is a sentiment analysis engine designed to gage the mood surronding various cryptocurrencies. Tweets are pulled for searched cryptocurrencies (e.g., bitcoin, ethereum, etc.) and parsed to sift out meaningful content. Next, the tweets are packaged and sent to IBM's Watson where natural language processing is performed. The results are displayed along with price in order to show the value / sentiment relationship.

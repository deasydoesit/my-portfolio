About Me

I am a software developer with experience in Java, C/C++, JavaScript, Node.js, and React.js, and have a passion for using my programming knowledge to bring ideas to life. In my previous role as a patent agent, I had the privilege of working with inventors at various Fortune 100 companies to draft and prosecute patent applications for their inventions in the computer arts. While I enjoyed the nuances and intricacies of patent law, I found myself becoming inspired by the inventors with whom I worked and increasingly captivated by the software development that goes into the materialization of inventions. In the next phase of my career, I seek a role as a software developer at a company where I can learn, grow, and continue to master the art of programming, as well as become an inventor myself.
